Pasta

Description

Perfect, peaceful, pristine, perky, picturesque, proper, pretty, priceless, pure pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 lb/500 g dried spaghetti
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 10 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp red chilli flakes
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley

Steps

  1. Cook 1lb/500g of dried spaghetti (or any other long dried pasta) in a large pot of salted boiling water per packet MINUS 1 minute.
  2. When pasta is ready, scoop out 1 cup of pasta cooking water, and drain pasta.
  3. Heat oil over medium high heat. Add garlic and cook until golden - be careful not to burn it.
  4. Toss with pasta and cooking water per Base Directions. Toss through parsley and chilli flakes, serve with parmesan.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Finish pasta per chosen recipe.
